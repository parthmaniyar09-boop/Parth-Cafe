Our Products

Baklava

Baklava is a rich, flaky pastry dessert originating from the Middle East and Mediterranean, featuring layers of filo dough, butter, and chopped nuts (pistachios, cashews, walnuts), sweetened with honey or syrup. It is known for its crispy texture, sweet taste, and is popular for celebrations like Ramadan, Eid, and Christmas.




Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a famous, no-bake Italian dessert known for its rich, creamy, and coffee-forward flavor profile. Its name translates to "pick me up" or "cheer me up" in Italian. It is traditionally made by dipping ladyfingers (savoiardi) in espresso and layering them with a mixture of mascarpone cheese, eggs, and sugar, topped with cocoa powder.
